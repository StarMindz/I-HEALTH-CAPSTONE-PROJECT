I-HEALTH OFFICIAL LAUNCH 2022

I-Health uses Blockchain technology to create a secure network that enable fast, and easy access to patients health records anywhere, anytime. It is an app for Hospitals to access patients’ health records checked in their domain using the patients’ Unique ID and a short authentication procedure
